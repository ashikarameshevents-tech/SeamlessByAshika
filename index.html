About

Hi, I’m Ashika. I bring a unique blend of global perspective and New York energy to wedding coordination. My passion for this industry was truly ignited during my own wedding journey. It was a beautiful masterclass that taught me an invaluable lesson: while planning is a joy, flawless execution is the heartbeat of a perfect day. Today, I combine those personal insights with my background in corporate strategy and operations.
