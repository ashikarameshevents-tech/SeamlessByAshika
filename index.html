About

Hi, I’m Ashika. I bring a unique blend of global perspective and New York energy to wedding coordination. My journey into this industry started with a deeply personal experience: planning my own wedding. While I found the planning process incredibly rewarding, on the actual day, much of my vision was not translated the way I had hoped—a feeling I never want another bride to experience. Drawing from my background in corporate strategy and operations, I now specialize in bringing high-level structure, calm communication, and flawless execution to complex wedding days.
